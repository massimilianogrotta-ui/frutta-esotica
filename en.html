February 2022: prices collapsed and contracts cancelled overnight across Eastern Europe. Goods in transit, client communicating they can no longer take the shipment.

→ The strategy we developed to protect ourselves: limited volumes and advance payments during high-risk periods.

FAQ

Still have questions?

Do I need to already work in the sector?

No. The guide is structured to be useful both to those starting from scratch and to those with some experience. The early chapters build the foundations; later chapters cover operational details that even professionals often handle poorly.

Does it cover products other than bananas?

Yes. Bananas are the reference product for many examples — because it is what the author knows best — but the supply chain, logistics, documentation and pricing principles apply to any exotic fruit: mango, avocado, pineapple, passion fruit and more.

Is the content up to date?

Yes. Duty rates, transit times and logistics costs are indicative and subject to change, but the operational structure and commercial logic described in the guide are timeless. Future updates are included with purchase.
